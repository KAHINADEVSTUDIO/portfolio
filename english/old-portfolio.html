Portfolio

This project of portfolio redesign was born from my desire to create an online space that reflects my skills, creativity and professional background.

By revisiting my old portfolio, I wanted to improve not only the aesthetics and user experience, but also to highlight my projects in a more dynamic and engaging way.

Goal of the project

The new version of my portfolio is a good example of my evolution as a developer, and also of my commitment to always improve my work.

Affirming my visual identity


Create a design that reflects my personal style and creativity, with a modern and professional aesthetic.

Improve user experience (UX)


Offer intuitive and pleasant navigation, allowing visitors to easily discover my work and skills.

Accessibility and adaptability


Ensure that the site is accessible to all, regardless of device or users' specific needs

Highlighting my achievements


Present my projects in an interactive and visually appealing way, with detailed descriptions and demonstrations.

Optimized performance


Maximize the speed and fluidity of the site to provide a flawless experience.

Logo

A key aspect of this redesign was the update of my visual identity, including the creation of a new logo. The logo is a central element that symbolizes the personality and values of my portfolio.

Before

Simple and minimalist logo, reflecting a sober but functional approach. It consisted of a basic typography with few graphic details, emphasizing legibility and simplicity.

After

Logo that embodies a more modern and refined identity. It has been designed to be more distinctive and memorable, with stylized typography and subtle graphic elements that evoke dynamism and creativity.

Redesign

Before

- Design: Simple and clean, but lacking dynamism and character.
- Features: Basic navigation with static structure and mainly textual information.
- Accessibility: Limited accessibility features, especially on mobile devices.

After

- Design: Modern with a thoughtful color scheme, elegant fonts, and subtle animations to energize the presentation.
- Features: Interactive sections, detailed portfolio with case studies and simplified navigation.
- Accessibility: Fully responsive site, optimized for use on all screen sizes, with special attention to accessibility standards.
